Career Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundations

Master the absolute essentials of modern software development, including core programming principles, data structures & algorithms (DSA), version control with Git, and fundamental database concepts.

Phase 2: Specialization

Choose your path and dive deep. Focus on building expertise in areas like Web Development (Front-end/Back-end), Data Science (AI/ML), or Product Management.

Phase 3: Growth

Apply your skills by building robust projects for your portfolio. Focus on professional networking, resume building, and rigorous interview preparation to land your dream role.
